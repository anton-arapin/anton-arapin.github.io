About Me

Hi! My name is Anton, I currently work as a Software Engineer at Google, and also pursue academic research. My interests lie in the fields of Computer Vision and Decision Making. In particular, I am most interested in using generative approaches to build novel human-centered systems, as well as developing agents that laverage uncertainty in novel environments!

I have worked on research projects at the GRASP Lab at the University of Pennsylvania under supervison of Dr. Kostas Daniilidis, and collaborate with Dr. Bernadette Bucher (Now at University of Michigan). I also collaborate with the research group of Dr. Junchen Jiang at the University of Chicago.

The main focus of my research has been the use of uncertainty and generative models in two fields: live video codecs and autonomus agent navigation in novel environments.
